Core Modules

Production-ready hiring operations across the entire cycle

Role and Vacancy Control

Manage open roles, headcount, hiring status, recruiter assignment, role details, and close-vacancy actions from one workspace.

Recruiter and Evaluator Coordination

Review recruiter requests, search evaluator options, open profiles, and run workflow actions including bulk evaluator assignment.

Candidate Pipeline Operations

Track candidates across screening, assessment, shortlist, hire, rejection, cancellation, and other role-specific decision stages.

Company and Hiring Identity

Edit company profile, branding, contact details, location, careers links, and workspace context directly from the hiring dashboard.

AI Talent Pool and Discovery

Use AI matching and search to move from flat candidate lists into stronger-fit role discovery and targeted talent review.

Exports, Auditability, and Reporting

Export candidate data to XLSX, Word, and PDF, surface analytics snapshots, and keep leadership reporting tied to the live pipeline.

Operating Model

From job publishing to candidate decision in one connected flow

01

Publish and structure hiring demand

Create roles, maintain company context, expose public jobs, and keep recruiter coverage attached to each open vacancy.

02

Capture candidates through multiple surfaces

Let candidates browse jobs, save roles, apply, manage profile data, refresh resume extraction, and monitor application stages.

03

Run evaluator and workflow operations

Schedule interviews, review pending recruiter requests, assign evaluators, search profiles, and move pipeline decisions forward.

04

Close the loop with AI and reporting

Use AI talent pool discovery, activity and analytics layers, risk indicators, and export-ready reports to drive decisions with context.
